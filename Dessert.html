Brownie

Baking brownie is rather easy once you got the ingredients down.

I love to bake brownie, whether it's from scratch or bought a brownie mix. I find baking a way to relax after a hard day of work and spend time with my siblings. I admit that it's really frustrating when my younger sister tells me what to do because I mess up a lot but it's good to have her around.

Benefits of baking

  • Spend time with family
  • Set your mind free of worries or anxiety
  • Be creative
  • Feed your stomach
  • Brings back old memories

BTS (Behind the scene)

Whenever my mom and I bake together, she always brings back old memories from her childhood when she baked sweets with her older siblings and nephews/nieces. A baking session with my mom is like getting to know more about her when she was younger or my older cousins through her stories. I don't know if anyone else likes to eat the raw batter but I do and that's the best part.
